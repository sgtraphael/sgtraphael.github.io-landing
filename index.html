About StraySafe

StraySafe is a community-driven mobile application designed to help reunite lost pets with their families in Hong Kong. Our platform leverages the power of community collaboration to create a comprehensive network for pet recovery.

Built with privacy and user safety in mind, StraySafe provides essential tools for pet owners while maintaining the highest standards of data protection and content moderation.

Whether you've lost a beloved pet or found a stray animal, StraySafe connects you with a caring community ready to help.
